2024 CDO Council Symposium

November 21st, 2024

The CDO Council hosted its first Data Symposium on Thursday, November 21, 2024 at the GSA Headquarters Auditorium, with the theme, "Data Powers Mission: Transforming Business and Mission with Data." As data is essential to every government function, the Federal Chief Data Officers (CDO) Council is committed to fostering collaboration, sharing best practices, and addressing federal data challenges. This fully in-person Symposium highlighted our dedication to positioning data as a strategic asset and explored innovative strategies for managing and leveraging data to achieve mission success across the Federal Enterprise.
